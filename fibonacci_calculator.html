The Fibonacci sequence, such that each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. That is,
Fibonacci(0) = 0
Fibonacci (1) = 1
Fibonacci (n) = Fibonacci (n-1) + Fibonacci (n-2)
